Our Story

A journey of tradition, passion, and the art of tea

Where Tradition Meets Heart

Jade Leaf Teahouse was born from a deep love for authentic Asian cuisine and the timeless ritual of tea culture. What started as a family dream has blossomed into a beloved gathering place where tradition and warmth come together.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that food is more than sustenance—it's a bridge between cultures, a connection between generations, and a celebration of craftsmanship. Every dumpling we fold, every tea we steep, and every dish we serve carries this philosophy.

"A single cup of tea can bring tranquility to the soul and connection to the heart."

Today, we continue to honor the heritage of Asian culinary arts while creating new memories for our community. Our teahouse is a sanctuary where time slows down, flavors tell stories, and every visit feels like coming home.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional recipes and techniques, using time-honored methods passed down through generations to create genuine flavors.

Quality First

From ceremonial grade matcha to the freshest ingredients, we never compromise on quality. Excellence is our standard in every dish.

Community

We're more than a restaurant—we're a gathering place where neighbors become friends and strangers become family.

Mindfulness

In our fast-paced world, we create a space for pause, reflection, and the simple joy of savoring the moment.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices, from sourcing ingredients responsibly to minimizing waste in our kitchen.

Love & Care

Every dish is prepared with genuine care and passion. We believe that love is the most important ingredient.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Jade Leaf Teahouse

2023

The Dream Begins

Our founders embarked on a journey across Asia, studying traditional tea ceremonies and authentic cooking techniques. This immersive experience planted the seed for what would become Jade Leaf.

Early 2024

Finding Our Home

We discovered the perfect location in Rocky Top, TN—a space that would become our sanctuary. Renovation began with great care to honor both traditional aesthetics and modern comfort.

Fall 2024

Grand Opening

Jade Leaf Teahouse opened its doors to the community. The overwhelming support and warm welcome from Rocky Top exceeded our wildest dreams.

2025

Growing Together

Today, we continue expanding our menu while staying true to our roots. We're honored to be part of Rocky Top's culinary landscape and look forward to many years of serving our community.
